# Business Problem

Organizations operating in regulated environments often manage thousands of documents across multiple repositories.

Without a centralized governance framework, document owners face challenges including:

* Missing review deadlines
* Inconsistent metadata
* Outdated document versions
* Lack of ownership accountability
* Limited auditability
* High administrative overhead

These challenges increase operational risk and make compliance management difficult.

The objective of the Enterprise Document Compliance Hub is to provide a centralized platform that standardizes document lifecycle management while reducing manual effort and improving governance controls.
