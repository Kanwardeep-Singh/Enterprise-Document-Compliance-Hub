# Solution Design Principles

## Overview

The solution was designed according to modern enterprise architecture principles to ensure maintainability, security, scalability, and governance.

---

# Principle 1: Security by Design

Security controls are embedded into every layer of the solution architecture.

---

# Principle 2: Configuration over Customization

Business rules should be configurable whenever possible.

---

# Principle 3: Reusability

Components, workflows, and governance patterns should be reusable across future initiatives.

---

# Principle 4: Scalability

The architecture must support growth in users, documents, and compliance requirements.

---

# Principle 5: Maintainability

Solutions should be understandable, supportable, and easy to enhance.

---

# Principle 6: Governance First

All functionality must align with organizational governance policies.

---

# Principle 7: User-Centric Design

The user experience should minimize complexity while maximizing productivity.

---

# Principle 8: Automation Wherever Practical

Manual activities should be replaced by governed automation whenever feasible.

---

# Expected Outcomes

These principles enable:

* Sustainable platform growth
* Improved compliance posture
* Reduced operational effort
* Better user adoption
* Stronger governance controls

The resulting architecture aligns with enterprise Power Platform and Microsoft 365 solution design best practices.
